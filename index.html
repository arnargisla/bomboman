Bomboman - a bomberman clone.
A small clone of bomberman with only one level. The most interesting part of this version is watching the ai battle it out. Set humans to 0 and computers to 4 and relax while the computer does the gaming for you. "Toggle render AI weights shows the weight for each square according to each AI (best viewed with only 1 AI). Trying to play the AI can be frusterating, as they are very efficiant at grabbing upgrades. (When it comes to destroying you they can be very dumb.
