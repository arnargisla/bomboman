Week 1

In the first week we will implement a functional basic version of bomberman.
Decide on art direction.

Week 2

In the second week we will create artwork for characters, mobs, powerups, 
tiles etc. We will also start finding/creating sounds for the game.
We will have a brainstorm session inwhere we try to come up with interesting 
novel features for our version of the game.

Week 3

In the third week we will fine-tune the artwork and sounds of the game. 
We might also implement any new features we have come up with or thought of.
